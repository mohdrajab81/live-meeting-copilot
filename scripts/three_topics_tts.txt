Topic 2: Marketing Plan for Launch
Now moving to marketing. The campaign will run in three phases: awareness, conversion, and retention. In the awareness phase, we will publish short product demos on LinkedIn and Instagram and work with two regional creators. In the conversion phase, we will run a seven-day promotion with a limited discount for first-time users. We also agreed to update the landing page headline to focus on time savings, because early tests showed that message performed better than price-based messaging. For retention, we will send onboarding emails on day 1, day 3, and day 7 with simple tips. Sara owns campaign execution, and she will share final creative assets by March 20. The analytics dashboard must be ready before launch so we can track signups, activation rate, and cost per acquisition in real time.
